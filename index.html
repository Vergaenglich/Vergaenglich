every moment is worth remembering.

Beauty is everywhere, if you slow down enough to notice it. In the decay and the rush, in the quiet street and the crowded square, in the remarkable face you pass without a second thought. We all move through the same world together. We just don't always stop or look at the same places. These photographs are about those moments in time of simple appreciation. If any of this resonates with you, I'd love to connect. Whether it's a project, a collaboration, or simply a photowalk through the city.

About

Photography as an act of noticing

This work is an appreciation for humanity — for the value of time, for the weight of a single moment that nobody paused to honour.

Shot across Berlin, Chicago, Havana, Paris and the Alps. Black and white, colour, grain and stillness. Every image is an argument against forgetting.

The camera is a Leica. The eye is human. The imperfections are intentional.
